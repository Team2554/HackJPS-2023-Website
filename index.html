About Us

HackJPS is a hackathon that is being led by students from JP Stevens High School. We are open to both high school and middle school students, and participation is not limited to those with a large background in programming - we hope to draw students from all skill levels, and even encourage participants who have not programmed before. Ultimately, HackJPS will be a really fun experience for students - participants will get a chance to make new friends with similar interests as well as to attend workshops hosted by our sponsors or relevant student organizations.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is an event where students passionate about STEM come together to create a “hack” in teams. This “hack” could be anything that is created from scratch between the time slot provided using a computer or technology. At the end of the timeframe, the best “hack” is chosen to be the winner of HackJPS!


Who can attend?

All high school and middle school students with an interest in STEM are welcome to attend!


How much does it cost?

The event is completely free to attend! Our amazing sponsors help us cover the cost to run HackJPS. You have nothing to lose if you attend.


When is it?

The event date has not been finalized yet. The exact date will be updated here and announced on Discord.


Do I need coding/hackathon experience to attend?

Everyone is welcome to participate, regardless of their coding experience. There will be many mentors and workshops to guide you along the way!


Do I need a team before I can sign up?

If you don’t have a team before the event, don’t worry! We encourage you to meet new students during or at the beginning of the event through Discord. The maximum number of hackers on a team is 5.


What can I win?

Prizes are to be determined.
